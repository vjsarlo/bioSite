MY HOBBIES
Where there is a city, there is a part that tourists don't visit. This is where Kaley wants to go. She wants to see the entire world as it really is, not how it is portrayed and sold to tourists.
Kaley's passion for food and travel comes from her hero, Anthony Bourdain. Anthony Bourdain taught her 3 things.
1. An incredible experience doesn't have to cost much
2. Savor the local culture where ever you are
3. Never take yourself - or food - too seriously
If Kaley is not traveling and eating food from parts unknown, she spends her time reading. Whether it be an Anthony Bourdain book, something about a new place she can go, or a novel she can get lost in, Kaley has a found admiration for taking moments to be quiet and read.
